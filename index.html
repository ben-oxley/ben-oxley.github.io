Oxley Cartography

Using high resolution LiDAR or Ordnance Survey data of the area to produce beautiful and fascinating 3D maps of the terrain around where you live. Ben got interested in the detail and patterns in geospatial data and started printing out landscapes to make wall art for himself and his family. Using open geospatial data, lidar, radar, photogrammetry, satellite data and more, Ben curates this data into 3D models and prints them in 3D. https://www.etsy.com/shop/oxleycartography
